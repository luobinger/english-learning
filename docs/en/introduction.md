An advanced guide to learn English which might benefit you a lot.

## Background

Hello friend, welcome to *English Level-Up Tips*.

When your eyes meet these words, I sincerely hope this is not just a grueling journey to conquer English, but a wondrous adventure that opens the door to wisdom. May these humble pages become a string of resonance between our hearts, playing the celestial melody of language learning.

Back in early July 2017, a friend named W., who was preparing for the TOEFL, asked me a question: **How can I efficiently learn English?**

As I pondered how to answer, I recalled my experience of passing 26 courses in a single semester during my senior year (19 of which were retakes, 7 were current), and that I had the fortune of ranking **first in my province** in both **English** and **Chinese** in the college entrance exam (Jiangsu Paper). Perhaps I was barely qualified to offer a few tips on efficient learning — if only as a modest contribution.

After exchanging study insights with her, I was surprised by her incredible enthusiasm for learning, while also noticing some areas where her approach could be improved.

So I wrote a brief article casually sharing my thoughts on learning English. A few days later, she told me she hoped I could organize these experiences and share them with more people in need.

Before that, I had no idea so many students struggled with learning English.

They had never even considered: **English, as a language, should be learned in a natural way — just as we naturally learned to speak Chinese**.

I genuinely hope everyone can **love** the process of learning English. If that's not possible, then try to discover the joy or benefits in it.

> The only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it.
>
> — Steve Jobs

**The same applies to learning**.

In this guide, I will combine my subjective views with scientific evidence as much as possible to provide a comprehensive English learning guide. **I sincerely hope this guide can be of some small help to you**.
