# AI Learning (2026 Edition)

> This chapter is updated based on online information as of 2026-03-20.

In the past two years, AI for learning English has evolved from "can chat with you a few sentences" to "can guide you step by step." If you only use it as a dictionary, translator, or essay polisher, you're only using a small part of its value.

What's more worth utilizing now is: it can help you build a micro-language environment close to real usage scenarios, continuously give you questions, correct errors, follow up with questions, review, and dynamically adjust difficulty based on your level.

---

## Why Gemini is Now Recommended First

Based on Google's official help center and product blog, Gemini is now most suitable for English learning, mainly for 5 reasons:

### 1. Emphasizes "Guided Learning"

Google released Guided Learning on 2025-08-06, explicitly emphasizing that it will help you understand through questioning, step-by-step breakdown, images, videos, and interactive quizzes, rather than just throwing answers at you.

This is especially important for English learning, because the biggest fear in learning a language is "understanding the answer, but not being able to use it yourself."

### 2. Native Support for Quizzes, Flashcards, and Study Guides

You can now directly feed articles, lectures, YouTube videos, or your own notes to Gemini and have it generate quizzes, flashcards, and study guides.

This means you can turn "reading an article" or "watching an English video" directly into follow-up review materials, rather than just finishing and ending.

### 3. Canvas is Great for Essay Correction and Expression Rewriting

Canvas is no longer just for generating text; it can also continue to rewrite, extend, shorten, adjust tone, and even convert materials into quizzes or audio overviews.

For English learning, it's particularly suitable for essay correction, email polishing, and oral outline organization.

### 4. Gemini Live is Great for Speaking Practice

The official help page clearly states that Gemini Live supports natural back-and-forth conversation, interruption, practicing important scenarios, and can even share camera and screen.

This means you can use it as a "sparring partner who can speak with you anytime," rather than just typing to practice English.

### 5. You Can Make It Your Own English Course

Gemini's Gems allow you to create a custom assistant with fixed teaching rules.

::: warning Note
Google's official help page clearly states that the preset Learning coach Gem currently does not support language learning, and Gems currently cannot be used directly with Gemini Live.
:::

## Building Your Gemini English Course

If you only plan to do one thing, do this first.

Don't start by asking: "Please help me learn English." This approach is too broad, and AI will only give you a bunch of correct but not very actionable suggestions.

A better approach is: first create a custom Gem in Gemini and fix it as your English coach.

You can directly use the following description as Gem instructions:

```
You are my English Level-Up Coach.
I am a native Chinese speaker, currently at about B1-B2 level in English, with a goal of significantly improving my overall listening, speaking, reading, and writing abilities within 12 weeks, prioritizing speaking, listening, and workplace expression.

Your task is not to give me a lot of advice at once, but to make English learning a course that can be sustainably advanced. Please follow these rules:
1) Default to interacting with me in English, but when explaining complex grammar, abstract word differences, or when I explicitly request, you can supplement with brief Chinese;
2) Each lesson should be controlled within 20-30 minutes;
3) Each lesson must include: warm-up, input material, output task, error correction feedback, review;
4) You should prioritize correcting high-frequency, transferable, and most commonly used errors in real scenarios, rather than being overly critical;
5) Each time give me only one clear small goal, such as "today only practice how to express disagreement more naturally";
6) You should continuously record my common errors and review them weekly;
7) If I say "start today's lesson," you should directly lead me into the next lesson;
8) If I upload articles, resumes, emails, meeting notes, or video subtitles, you should transform them into English course materials;
9) You should push me to actively output every time, don't give long speeches to complete learning for me;
10) After each lesson, output 4 items: expressions learned today, key errors I made, homework, and focus for next lesson.
```

## Other AI Tool Division of Labor

### ChatGPT

- Suitable for quick Q&A and brainstorming
- GPT-4o's multimodal capability can analyze English in images
- Suitable for vocabulary expansion and example sentence generation

### Claude

- Strong long-text processing ability
- Suitable for correcting long articles
- Excellent logical analysis ability

### Perplexity

- Suitable for finding the latest learning resources
- Can search and summarize English learning methods
- Provides citation sources, more reliable information

### DeepL Write

- Focuses on writing polishing
- Provides more authentic expression suggestions
- Suitable for formal writing like emails and reports

## AI Learning Workflow

### Speaking Practice

1. Use Gemini Live for conversation practice
2. Upload topics and let AI generate discussion questions
3. Let AI analyze pronunciation and grammar issues after recording

### Listening Training

1. Upload audio/video subtitles
2. Let AI generate comprehension questions
3. Use AI to explain new words and expressions

### Reading Improvement

1. Upload English articles
2. Let AI generate vocabulary lists and comprehension questions
3. Use AI to explain difficult sentences and cultural background

### Writing Improvement

1. Let AI correct after writing
2. Let AI provide more authentic expressions
3. Use AI to generate model essays on similar topics

---

::: tip AI Learning Advice
1. **Don't rely on AI**: AI is a tool, not a substitute
2. **Active output**: Let AI push you to speak and write, not just look at answers
3. **Regular review**: Use AI-generated review materials to regularly consolidate
4. **Diversify usage**: Different tools have different advantages, combined use is more effective
:::
